A regular expression can consist of the following constructions:

    • It can match a symbol c from the alphabet of the language to be matched.
      It can match either of two regular expressions r1 and r2, written r1|r2.
      It can match a sequence of two regular expressions r1 and r2, written r1r2.
      It can match zero or more instances of a regular expression r, written r*.
